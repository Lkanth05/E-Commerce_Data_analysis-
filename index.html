Dataset Summary

This comprehensive e-commerce dataset provides insights into customer behavior across 100,000 user interactions from 10,000 unique customers. The analysis reveals strong performance with a conversion rate of 16.20% and an average order value of $479.32. Electronics categories (Tablets, Laptops, Smartphones) dominate revenue generation, accounting for over $2M in combined sales. The data shows healthy engagement patterns with 70% of events being product views, indicating strong browsing behavior that converts effectively to purchases.
